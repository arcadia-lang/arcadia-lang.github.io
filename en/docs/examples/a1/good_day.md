# A good day

## Passage

vidivo veon in la bazare.
obvendivi yes pomeson.
parlivo "salvuful", ma ne audivi meon.
poste, convidivas intra la parce.
risivi, i parlivas.
sive ye die boni.

## Translation

I saw you at the market.
You bought apples.
I said "hello", but you did not hear me.
Later, we met at the park.
You smiled, and we talked.
It was a good day.
