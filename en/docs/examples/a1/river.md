# A walk by the river

## Passage

save la solis lumini.
pasave ye pueris ad la fluve.
vidave ye peskon magni.
saltave le peskis.
ridave le pueris.
andarave ad la dome laeti.

## Translation

The sun is bright.
A child walks to the river.
They see a big fish.
The fish jumps.
The child laughs.
They go home happy.

## Breakdown

1. _save la solis lumini._
   - **translation:** The sun is bright.
1. _pasave ye pueris ad la fluve._
   - **translation:** A child walks to the river.
1. _vidave ye peskon magni._
   - **translation:** They see a big fish.
1. _saltave le peskis._
   - **translation:** The fish jumps.
   - **notes:** We use the anaphoric article "le" to refer to the fish.
1. _ridave le pueris._
   - **translation:** The child laughs.
1. _andarave ad la dome laeti._
   - **translation:** They go home happy.
