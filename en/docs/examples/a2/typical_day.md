# A typical day

This passage introduces daily routine vocabulary and the iterative aspect.

=== "Arcadia"

    movavume la solis adhlim on la matine.
    cantavumes es avisis aut de me fenestre.
    potavumo a teon i edravumo a panon ja a matitxene.
    pasavume de me vecinis con de asé canis epi de me dome.
    on la vespere, jogavumes es pueresis on la vie.
    volaudhavumo multie misme canton.
    venavume la noctis, i apparavume las stellesis.

=== "English"

    The sun rises in the morning.
    Birds sing outside my window.
    I drink tea and eat bread for breakfast.
    My neighbour walks with his dog in front of my house.
    In the evening, children play in the street.
    I listen to the same song many times.
    Night comes, and the stars appear.

=== "Explanation"

    1. _movavume la solis adhlim on la matine._
        - **translation:** The sun rises in the morning.
    1. _cantavumes es avisis aut de me fenestre._
        - **translation:** Birds sing outside my window.
    1. _potavumo a teon i edravumo a panon ja a matitxene._
        - **translation:** I drink tea and eat bread for breakfast.
    1. _pasavume de me vecinis con de asé canis epi de me dome._
        - **translation:** My neighbour walks with his dog in front of my house.
    1. _on la vespere, jogavumes es pueresis on la vie._
        - **translation:** In the evening, children play in the street.
    1. _volaudhavumo multie misme canton._
        - **translation:** I listen to the same song many times.
    1. _venavume la noctis, i apparavume las stellesis._
        - **translation:** Night comes, and the stars appear.
