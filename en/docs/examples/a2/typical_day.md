# A typical day

## Passage

umsurgeve la solis cui matine.
canteves yes avisis apud de me fenestre.
potevo chaion i edrevo panon pro matichene.
andareve de me vecinis cum de ase canis ante me dome.
in vespere, joveves es pueresis intra la vie.
volaudevo multie misme canton.
veneve las noctis, i appareve las stellesis.

## Translation

The sun rises every morning.
Birds sing near my window.
I drink tea and eat bread for breakfast.
My neighbour walks with his dog in front of my house.
In the evening, children play in the street.
I listen to the same song many times.
Night comes, and the stars appear.
