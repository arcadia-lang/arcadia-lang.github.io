# Nouns

Nouns are a **foundational element** of Arcadia's grammar.
They carry many of the language's complexities, making them an essential starting point for understanding Arcadia.

Arcadian nouns have **five [numbers][number]**, and **seven [cases][case]**.
The order of affixes follows this structure: **gender - base - number - case**.

The **[gender][gender]** and **number** of a noun convey **semantic meaning**, while **case** functions purely as a **syntactic marker**.
