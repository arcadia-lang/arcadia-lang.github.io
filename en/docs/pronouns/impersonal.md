# Impersonal Pronouns

Arcadia has **two impersonal pronouns**, **"le"** and **"alé"**, which differ in **degree of specificity**.

- **"je"** → Represents an **implied but relatively specific** vague subject (e.g., a known general group).
- **"ajé"** → Represents a **fully indeterminate** or **diffuse** subject (e.g., society as a whole).

## **Impersonal Pronoun Declensions**

Both **"je"** and **"ajé"** follow standard noun declension patterns.
