# Impersonal Pronouns

Arcadia has **two impersonal pronouns**, **"le"** and **"alé"**, which differ in **degree of specificity**.

- **"le"** → Represents an **implied but relatively specific** vague subject (e.g., a known general group).
- **"alé"** → Represents a **fully indeterminate** or **diffuse** subject (e.g., society as a whole).

---

## **Impersonal Pronoun Declensions**

Both **"le"** and **"alé"** follow standard noun declension patterns.
