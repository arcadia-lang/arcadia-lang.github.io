# Questions

Questions in Arcadia are formed using a **question particle**, followed by sentence **with the unknown part omitted**.

- **Yes/no questions** → Indicated by **rising intonation** in spoken Arcadia.
- **Open-ended questions** → Use **interrogative particles**, which combine with **prepositions and declensions** as expected.

## **Question Particles**

| **Particle** | **English**       | **Meaning**      |
| ------------ | ----------------- | ---------------- |
| **ku**       |                   | Yes/no question  |
| **ke**       | Who/what          | Generic inquiry  |
| **kuál**     | Which             | Choice selection |
| **kove**     | Where             | Location-based   |
| **kuandho**  | When              | Time reference   |
| **kuót**     | How much/how many | Quantity inquiry |
| **kume**     | How               | Manner inquiry   |

- **Interrogative words can combine with prepositions**, allowing **expanded meanings**:
    - _pro ke_ → "Why"
    - _por ke_ → "For whose benefit"

!!! note "Handling Unknowns"

    In Arcadia, asking a question **causes affected suffixes to drop**:

        - **"kuót"** removes **quantity markers**, simplifying nouns.
        - **"kuandho"** drops **time suffixes** when asking about duration.
        - **"ke"** removes **person suffixes** when querying the subject of a verb.

## **Examples**

- **Yes/No Question:**
    - _ku donavi ta librion meil?_ → **"Are you giving the book to me?"**

- **Method Inquiry:**
    - _kume donavi ta librion meil?_ → **"How are you giving the book to me?"**
        - **Explanation:** Asking about the method—e.g., by hand, by post.

- **Location Inquiry:**
    - _kove donavi ta librion meil?_ → **"Where are you giving the book to me?"**

- **Subject Inquiry:**
    - _keil donavi ta librion?_ → **"To whom are you giving the book?"**
    - _keon donavi meil?_ → **"What are you giving to me?"**

- **Specific Selection:**
    - _kuál librion donavi meil?_ → **"Which book are you giving to me?"**

- **Temporal Reference:**
    - _kuandho donovi ta librion meil?_ → **"When are you going to give the book to me?"**
        - **Explanation:** Implies it is **in the future**.

- **Quantity Inquiry:**
    - _kuót librion donavi meil?_ → **"How many books are you giving to me?"**

### **Asking for the Subject of the Verb**

- **Keeps only the known parts**, ensuring clarity in reference.

| **Sentence**                     | **Translation**                                    | **Explanation**                                                   |
| -------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------- |
| _keis donav ta librion meil?_    | "Who is giving the book to me?"                    | No indication about **who or how many** are involved.             |
| _kesis donaves ta librion meil?_ | "Who is giving the book to me?"                    | **Third-person plural**—multiple people, **excluding you or me**. |
| _kesis donavis ta librion meil?_ | "Who among you (plural) is giving the book to me?" | Used as a **follow-up question** when the subject is unclear.     |

## **Common Questions**

- _kei seve de ve namis?_ → **"What's your name?"**
- _kume savi?_ → **"How are you?"**
- _eks cove sevi?_ → **"Where are you from?"**
