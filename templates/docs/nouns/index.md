# <x-trans>Nouns</x-trans>

<x-trans>Nouns are a **foundational element** of Arcadia's grammar.</x-trans>
<x-trans>They carry many of the language's complexities, making them an essential starting point for understanding Arcadia.</x-trans>

<x-trans>Arcadian nouns have **five [numbers][&{number}]**, and **seven [cases][&{case}]**.</x-trans>
<x-trans>The order of affixes follows this structure: **gender - base - number - case**.</x-trans>

<x-trans>The **[gender][&{gender}]** and **number** of a noun convey **semantic meaning**, while **case** functions purely as a **syntactic marker**.</x-trans>
