# <x-trans>Personal Pronouns</x-trans>

<x-trans>Arcadia recognizes **three core personal pronouns**, corresponding to **first, second, and third persons**.</x-trans>
<x-trans>The **fourth person** serves a **generalized reference**, expressing **all objects or none**—it is handled through **indefinite pronouns**, rather than a distinct personal form.</x-trans>

## **<x-trans>Pronoun Overview</x-trans>**

| **<x-trans>Person</x-trans>** | **<x-trans>Pronoun</x-trans>** |
| ----------------------------- | ------------------------------ |
| **<x-trans>1st</x-trans>**    | me                             |
| **<x-trans>2nd</x-trans>**    | ve                             |
| **<x-trans>3rd</x-trans>**    | asé                            |
