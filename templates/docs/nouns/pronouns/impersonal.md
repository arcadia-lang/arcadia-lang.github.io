# <x-trans>Impersonal Pronouns</x-trans>

<x-trans>Arcadia has **two impersonal pronouns**, **"&{le}"** and **"&{alé}"**, which differ in **degree of specificity**.</x-trans>

- [x] **"le"** → <x-trans>Represents an **implied but relatively specific** vague subject (e.g., a known general group).</x-trans>
- [x] **"alé"** → <x-trans>Represents a **fully indeterminate** or **diffuse** subject (e.g., society as a whole).</x-trans>

---
