# <x-trans>Pronouns</x-trans>

<x-trans>Arcadia categorizes pronouns into four distinct types:</x-trans>

- **[<x-trans>Personal pronouns</x-trans>][personal]** – <x-trans>Used for referring to individuals in speech and writing.</x-trans>
- **[<x-trans>Impersonal pronouns</x-trans>][impersonal]** – <x-trans>Express general or indefinite subjects.</x-trans>
- **[<x-trans>Demonstrative pronouns</x-trans>][demonstrative]** – <x-trans>Indicate specific objects or concepts in relation to space or discourse.</x-trans>
- **[<x-trans>Interrogative pronouns</x-trans>][interrogative]** – <x-trans>Used to ask questions about **identity, quantity, selection, or characteristics** of a noun.</x-trans>
