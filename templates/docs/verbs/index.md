# Verbs

Arcadia's verbs **dictate sentence structure**, shaping **main syntax** based on **verb type**.
They convey extensive grammatical information through a **prefix and suffix system**, integrating **mood, tense, aspect, and person distinctions**.

### **Core Verb Features**

- **[Verb types][type]** → Define sentence structure and determine **syntactic dependencies**.
- **[Moods][mood]** → Arcadia has **8 moods**, allowing precise expression of **intent and modality**.
- **[Tenses][tense]** → Arcadia distinguishes **5 tenses**, structuring actions across **time frames**.
- **[Aspects][aspect]** → With **9 aspects**, verbs encode **action flow and duration**.
- **[Persons][person]** → Due to **nullar and collective numbers**, Arcadia accommodates **4 grammatical persons**.
